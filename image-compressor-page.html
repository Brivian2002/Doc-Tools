About Image Compression

Our image compressor uses advanced algorithms to reduce the file size of your images while maintaining visual quality. This is perfect for:

  • Optimizing images for your website to improve loading speed
  • Reducing email attachment sizes
  • Saving storage space on your devices
  • Preparing images for social media uploads

We support JPEG, PNG, GIF, and other common image formats. Your files remain private and are processed entirely in your browser - nothing is uploaded to our servers.
